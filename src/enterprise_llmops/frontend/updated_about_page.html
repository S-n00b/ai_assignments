Model Factory Architecture for Enterprise Scale

A "model factory" is a data management architecture and MLOps framework that automates the end-to-end machine learning (ML) lifecycle to develop, deploy, and manage a vast number of ML models at scale. For companies like Lenovo, this approach is crucial for consistently deploying ML models across a diverse ecosystem of devices, from resource-intensive servers to memory-constrained mobile phones and IoT devices.

Key architectural components and practices like a model factory contribute to high success by ensuring consistency and performance across all products and devices.
