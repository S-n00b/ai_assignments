The "Gold Net" Vision: Shared Network Intelligence

The true power emerges when users opt-in to share their networks with trusted connections. This creates the "Gold Net"—a collective intelligence where every participant gains access to exponentially more connections while maintaining strict privacy controls and compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.).

The Gold Net is powered by a Cypher-based graph database (e.g., Neo4j) designed for complex, multi-hop network traversals. This enables users to find connections through multiple degrees of separation while maintaining privacy and compliance standards.
